BuyBye

Centralized Grocery Price Comparison Platform for Pakistan

BB

Abstract

BuyBye revolutionizes grocery shopping in Pakistan by offering a unified digital platform that delivers real-time price comparison across local vendors. It tackles price opacity, time-consuming manual comparisons, and limited vendor visibility.

Introduction

Grocery shoppers often waste time comparing prices across multiple stores, while small vendors lack an online presence to reach new customers. BuyBye centralizes vendor data for informed decisions.

Objectives

  • Enable real-time, multi-vendor price comparison
  • Implement geolocation-based vendor discovery
  • Offer a custom ration-pack builder
  • Provide AI-powered recipe suggestions
  • Empower local vendors with online tools
  • Design a scalable, secure architecture
